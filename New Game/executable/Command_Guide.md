# SurvivalRPG Command Guide

Welcome to the world of SurvivalRPG! This guide provides a comprehensive overview of the commands you can use to interact with the game world, manage your character, and survive the challenges ahead.

## Movement

*   **`go [direction]`**: Navigate the world by moving your character. Replace `[direction]` with `north`, `south`, `east`, or `west`.
    *   Example: `go north`

## Interaction & Exploration

*   **`look`**: Get a detailed description of your current location, including any items, structures, or enemies present.
*   **`take [item]`**: Pick up an item from the ground or from an open container. Replace `[item]` with the name of the item you wish to acquire.
    *   Example: `take rusty knife`
*   **`drop [item]`**: Leave an item from your inventory at your current location.
    *   Example: `drop old rags`
*   **`open [container]`** or **`search [container]`**: Interact with containers or objects in the environment to discover their contents.
    *   Example: `search fridge`
*   **`use [item]`**: Consume or activate an item from your inventory. This can include healing items, sanity-restoring items, or special artifacts.
    *   Example: `use bandage`

## Character Management

*   **`inventory`** or **`i`**: View all items currently in your possession.
*   **`status`**: Display your character's current health, hunger, thirst, psychological state (sanity, psychosis, fear, paranoia), and equipped items.
*   **`equip [item]`**: Move an item from your inventory to an appropriate equipment slot (e.g., weapon to main hand, clothing to torso).
    *   Example: `equip sharpened spear`
*   **`unequip [slot]`**: Remove an item from an equipment slot and place it back into your inventory. Replace `[slot]` with the name of the slot (e.g., `main hand`, `torso`).
    *   Example: `unequip main hand`
*   **`wield [torch]`**: Specifically equip a torch, providing light and potentially other benefits.
    *   Example: `wield torch`

## Combat & Crafting

*   **`attack [enemy] [limb]`**: Engage in combat with an enemy. You can optionally specify a `[limb]` to target for strategic attacks (e.g., `head`, `legs`, `arms`, `torso`).
    *   Example: `attack zombie head`
*   **`build [structure]`**: Construct a new structure at your current location, provided you have the necessary materials and knowledge.
    *   Example: `build campfire`
*   **`craft [item]`**: Create new items from raw materials using known recipes.
    *   Example: `craft bandage`

## Allies

*   **`recruit [survivor]`**: Convince a non-player character (NPC) to join your cause and potentially move to your safehouse.
    *   Example: `recruit lone survivor`

## Game Management

*   **`save`**: Preserve your current game progress.
*   **`quit`**: Exit the game. Be sure to save first if you wish to continue your adventure later.

Remember to experiment with commands and explore the world to uncover all its secrets. Good luck, survivor!
